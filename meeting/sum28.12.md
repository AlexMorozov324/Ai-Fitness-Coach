## Key decision: project edge & main feature
Our main differentiator will be a **real-time exercise tracking and form 
validation feature**:

- Track the user while they perform exercises.
- Detect whether movements are being executed correctly.
- Provide **instant corrections** when the form is wrong (e.g., posture, 
range of motion, timing).
- Provide **motivation and encouragement** when needed to help the user 
stay consistent.

This feature will be the **central pillar** of the project.
